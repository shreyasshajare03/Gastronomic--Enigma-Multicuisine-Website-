Udon Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces udon noodles
  • 4 cups dashi (Japanese soup stock)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons mirin
  • 2 tablespoons sake
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 cup sliced shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 cup baby spinach leaves
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Tempura flakes or chopped nori (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the udon noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, combine the dashi, soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar. Bring to a simmer over medium heat.
  3. Add the sliced shiitake mushrooms to the pot and simmer for 2-3 minutes until they are tender.
  4. Add the cooked udon noodles to the pot and stir gently to combine. Simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  5. Stir in the baby spinach leaves and cook until wilted.
  6. Divide the udon soup into bowls. Garnish with sliced green onions and, if desired, sprinkle with tempura flakes or chopped nori.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade udon!

Feel free to customize the recipe based on your preferences!
