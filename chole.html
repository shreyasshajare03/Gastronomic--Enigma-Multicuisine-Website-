Chole Bhature Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chickpeas (chole), soaked overnight
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon chole masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Boil the soaked chickpeas until tender and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, tomato puree, and cook until the oil separates.
  6. Stir in turmeric powder, chole masala, red chili powder, and salt.
  7. Add the boiled chickpeas and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  8. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.

Bhature:

  1. Ingredients for Bhature:
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 cup yogurt
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1 tablespoon sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • Water (as needed to make the dough)
    • Oil for frying
  2. Instructions for Bhature:
    • Mix all-purpose flour, yogurt, baking powder, baking soda, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
    • Add water gradually to form a smooth and soft dough.
    • Cover the dough and let it rest for at least 2 hours.
    • Divide the dough into small balls and roll them into rounds.
    • Heat oil in a pan and fry the bhature until they puff up and turn golden brown.
    • Serve hot with chole.

Enjoy your delicious Chole Bhature!
