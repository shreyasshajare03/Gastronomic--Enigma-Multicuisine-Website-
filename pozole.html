Pozole Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds pork shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (15 oz each) hominy, drained and rinsed
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 6 cups chicken or pork broth
  • For garnish: chopped radishes, shredded cabbage, chopped cilantro, lime wedges, and sliced jalapeños

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine the pork chunks, chopped onion, minced garlic, oregano, cumin, chili powder, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Cook over medium heat until the pork is browned on all sides and the onions are softened.
  3. Add the drained hominy and chicken or pork broth to the pot. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours until the pork is tender.
  4. Skim off any excess fat that rises to the surface during cooking.
  5. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  6. Serve the pozole hot, garnished with chopped radishes, shredded cabbage, chopped cilantro, lime wedges, and sliced jalapeños.

Enjoy your delicious Pozole!
