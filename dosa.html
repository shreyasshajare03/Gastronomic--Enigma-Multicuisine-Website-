Dosa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram lentils)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (for soaking and grinding)
  • Oil or ghee (for cooking)

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds together. Soak them in water for at least 4-6 hours or overnight.
  2. After soaking, drain the water and grind the rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds to a smooth batter. Add water as needed to achieve the right consistency. The batter should be slightly thick.
  3. Add salt to the batter and mix well. Allow the batter to ferment for 8-12 hours or overnight. The batter should rise and become slightly fluffy.
  4. Heat a dosa griddle or a non-stick pan. Grease it with a little oil or ghee.
  5. Pour a ladle full of batter onto the hot griddle and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  6. Drizzle some oil or ghee around the edges of the dosa. Cook until the edges turn golden brown, and the dosa is crisp.
  7. Fold the dosa and serve hot with coconut chutney, sambar, or your favorite accompaniment.

Enjoy your delicious Dosa!
