Culinary Traditions

Italian Cuisine

Italian cuisine is a style of cooking that originated in the Italian peninsula. It has evolved over centuries, influenced by regional variations, traditions, and political events.

French Cuisine

French cuisine is the traditional cuisine of France. It has developed over centuries and evolved considerably. French cooking uses fresh, high-quality ingredients.

Chinese Cuisine

Chinese cuisine, a broad category encompassing Chinese cooking traditions, techniques, ingredients, flavours, and food preparation methods, originated in the Zhou Dynasty of ancient China.

Indian Cuisine

Indian cuisine refers to the diverse and varied dishes of the cuisine of the Republic of India. India is known for its rich cultural heritage, strong social traditions and religious pilgrimage destinations, resulting in a vast array of different dishes and flavors.
