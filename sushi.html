Sushi Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Nori (seaweed) sheets
  • Sushi-grade fish (such as tuna or salmon), thinly sliced
  • Vegetables (such as cucumber, avocado, and carrot), julienned
  • Soy sauce, for dipping
  • Wasabi and pickled ginger, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Combine the rice and water in a rice cooker and cook according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  2. In a small saucepan, combine the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Heat over low heat until the sugar and salt dissolve. Allow the mixture to cool.
  3. Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a large bowl and gently fold in the vinegar mixture, being careful not to smash the rice grains. Let the rice cool to room temperature.
  4. Place a bamboo sushi rolling mat on a clean surface. Lay a sheet of nori, shiny side down, on the mat.
  5. With wet hands, spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving about 1 inch at the top edge of the nori uncovered.
  6. Place slices of sushi-grade fish and julienned vegetables along the bottom edge of the rice.
  7. Roll the sushi tightly from the bottom using the bamboo mat as a guide. Seal the edge with a little water.
  8. Use a sharp knife to slice the roll into bite-sized pieces.
  9. Repeat the process with different combinations of fish and vegetables to make various sushi rolls.
  10. Serve the sushi with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Enjoy your homemade sushi!
