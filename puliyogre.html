Puliyogare Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup tamarind extract (thick)
  • 1 cup rice, cooked and cooled
  • 1/4 cup peanuts, roasted
  • 1/4 cup sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon chana dal (split chickpeas)
  • 1 teaspoon urad dal (black gram lentils)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida (hing)
  • 2-3 dried red chilies
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 2 tablespoons jaggery, grated
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chana dal, urad dal, fenugreek seeds, dried red chilies, and asafoetida. Saut√© until the dals turn golden brown.
  3. Add curry leaves and turmeric powder. Stir well.
  4. Pour in the tamarind extract and bring it to a boil. Add salt and grated jaggery. Simmer until the mixture thickens.
  5. Add the cooked rice to the tamarind mixture. Mix well to coat the rice evenly.
  6. Stir in the roasted peanuts. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend.
  7. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly before serving.
  8. Serve Puliyogare with a side of yogurt or enjoy it on its own.

Enjoy your delicious Puliyogare!
