Butter Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, marinate the chicken with yogurt, garam masala, chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add chopped onions, garlic, and grated ginger. Saut√© until the onions are golden brown.
  3. Add the marinated chicken to the pan. Cook until the chicken is browned on all sides.
  4. Stir in tomato puree and ground cumin. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  5. Pour in the heavy cream and cook for an additional 5 minutes, ensuring the chicken is cooked through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.

Enjoy your delicious Butter Chicken!
