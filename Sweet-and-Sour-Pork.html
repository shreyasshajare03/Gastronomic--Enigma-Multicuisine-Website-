Sweet and Sour Pork Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pork tenderloin, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks (fresh or canned)
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 green bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, cut into chunks
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • For the Sweet and Sour Sauce:
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch (optional, for thickening)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the ingredients for the sweet and sour sauce: ketchup, rice vinegar, brown sugar, and soy sauce. If you prefer a thicker sauce, dissolve cornstarch in a little water and add it to the mixture. Set aside.
  2. In separate bowls, place flour and beaten eggs.
  3. Dip the pork pieces into the flour, then into the beaten eggs, ensuring they are fully coated.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat. Fry the coated pork pieces until golden brown and cooked through. Remove and set aside on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  5. In the same pan, saut√© the bell peppers and onions until they are slightly tender.
  6. Add the pineapple chunks to the pan and pour the sweet and sour sauce over the vegetables. Stir to combine.
  7. Return the fried pork to the pan, coating it with the sweet and sour sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through.
  8. Serve the Sweet and Sour Pork over cooked rice.

Enjoy your delicious Sweet and Sour Pork!
