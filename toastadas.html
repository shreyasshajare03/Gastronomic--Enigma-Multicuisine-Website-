Tostadas Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 can (15 oz) refried beans
  • 1 lb ground beef or shredded chicken (seasoned with taco seasoning)
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Salsa and hot sauce for topping (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the corn tortillas on a baking sheet and bake in the oven for 8-10 minutes or until they are crispy and golden brown. Alternatively, you can fry them in oil until crisp.
  3. In a skillet, heat the refried beans over medium heat until warmed through.
  4. In another skillet, cook the ground beef or shredded chicken with taco seasoning until fully cooked and well-seasoned.
  5. Assemble the tostadas: Spread a layer of refried beans on each crispy tortilla. Top with the seasoned meat, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream, sliced avocado, and chopped cilantro.
  6. Squeeze lime wedges over the tostadas and, if desired, add salsa and hot sauce for extra flavor.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious tostadas!

Feel free to customize the recipe based on your preferences!
